France

France is a place that almost everyone would say they would love to visit. Especially for my mom and sister its a dream for the both of them to go on vacation over there. Home of the baguette France is well known for the bread they produce and have been making for a long time since Louis XIV. Its also home to one of the most recognizable landmarks the Eiffel Tower. Its the biggest country in the EU and has over 27 states to visit. France also uses 12 different time zones across the country.

Paris

Home of the world famous Eiffel Tower Paris is a staple visit if you go to France. Being the capital of France it is one of the most popular tourist destinations and a typical go to for most families, couples, and more. The Eiffel Tower is the go to attraction in Paris having visitors climb 1,665 steps to reach the top or using the elevator, personally for me I would use the elevator cause thats a lot of steps. Cheese sampling is also a common activity for visitors with over 1000 different types of cheese for you cheese lovers to try.
