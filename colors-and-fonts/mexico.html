Mexico

Mexico is the only place I have traveled to out of these 3 locations and in general out of the US. Since my dad is from Mexico we have been over there multiple times for different reasons from visiting my dad’s side of the family to just going for vacation. We usually stay at my grandparents house and do activities around the area. Typically when we go over there always seems to be a family party that we end up attending. Even though my parents main language is spanish I don’t speak it frequently at all so when we end up going over there I always have to have my parents talk for me because I don’t know spanish well. A fact about Mexico is that there are 31 states.

Cancún

Cancun for me is a more enjoyable experience since there are lots of activities I enjoy doing offered at the hotels we stay at. I have only been there twice but both times were really enjoyable. Some of my favorite activities in Cancun that I have done were deep sea fishing, jet skiing, and just visiting the beach. Fishing is probably my most favorite activity besides playing video games so getting the opportunity to go deep sea fishing is awesome cause I get the chance at catching fish that normally I would never see because of the area we live in. A fact about cancun is that it has over 1.3 billion gallons of sand along the coast.
