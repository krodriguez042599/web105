Why build a PC?

  • Building a PC is more affordable.
  • Being able to pick every component in your system.
  • You get to learn what goes into a PC and how they come together to become a working system.
  • Since you assembled it, it will be easier for the future to take apart and upgrade your parts.
  • Its a rewarding feeling after completing the build.

    • My Experince
